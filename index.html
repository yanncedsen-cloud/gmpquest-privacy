Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 13, 2025

Privacy-First Design: GMPQuest is designed with your privacy as a top priority. All your data stays on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

1. Information Collection

GMPQuest operates entirely on your device with no data collection. Specifically:

  • No personal information collected: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information.
  • No user accounts: GMPQuest does not require registration, login, or authentication of any kind.
  • No tracking or analytics: We do not use analytics services, tracking pixels, or any monitoring tools.
  • No advertising: GMPQuest contains no advertisements and does not share data with advertising networks.

2. Local Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device only:

  • User profiles: Profile names and preferences are stored on your device using iOS AsyncStorage.
  • Learning progress: Your quiz scores, XP points, completed modules, and learning statistics remain on your device.
  • Settings and preferences: Theme preferences and app settings are saved locally.
  • No cloud backup: Your data is never synced to cloud servers or backed up outside your device.

Important: If you delete the app or reset your device, all data will be permanently lost as it is not stored anywhere else.

3. Educational Content Delivery

GMPQuest downloads educational content from our servers to provide learning materials:

  • Quiz questions: Questions and answers are downloaded from our server.
  • GMP regulations: Regulatory reference materials (FDA, EMA, ICH guidelines) are fetched from our content database.
  • No user data sent back: When downloading content, we do not send any user information, answers, scores, or personal data to our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

GitHub Pages Hosting: This privacy policy is hosted on GitHub Pages. When you visit this page, GitHub may collect visitor IP addresses for security purposes. For more information, see GitHub's Privacy Statement.

Apple App Store: GMPQuest is distributed through the Apple App Store. Apple may collect information related to app downloads and updates according to their privacy policy. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID).
  • We recommend enabling device encryption and using a secure passcode.
  • Network communications use HTTPS encryption when downloading educational content.

6. Children's Privacy

GMPQuest is designed for pharmaceutical professionals and is rated for ages 13+. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Since the app operates entirely on-device with no data collection, children's privacy is inherently protected.

7. Your Rights

Because GMPQuest does not collect or store any personal data on servers, traditional data rights (access, deletion, portability) are not applicable. You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete your data: Simply delete the app from your device to remove all associated data.
  • Reset progress: Use the profile reset feature within the app to clear your learning progress while keeping the app installed.
  • Switch profiles: Create multiple profiles to separate different learning sessions.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

If we make material changes to how we handle user data (such as introducing data collection), we will notify users through the app and obtain consent where required by law.

9. International Users

GMPQuest can be used worldwide. Since all data is stored locally on your device, there are no international data transfers or cross-border data processing concerns.
